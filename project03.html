About this Design

This design is a multi-themed cookie package product to be sold in stores and marketplaces. The velvet-flavored cookie is smooth and sweet, and the packaging was developed to embody the smoothness - a sleek, informative branding in mind to stand out on the large shelves of oversaturated cookie products.

Red in Riches

The color red is a very aggressive color, which is why it is important not to overwhelm the viewer with a very "in your face" color choice.

Meant to Stand Out

This package will stand out on any shelf in any market.

Design must stand out, there should be nothing left on the table in a design.
