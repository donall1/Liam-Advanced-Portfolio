About Pastel Pop

This design of a sparkling water soda product is intended to embody the light and healthy features that alternative water products offer. Light and airy colors, along with a soft design concept, create a soothing allure. The overall goal of this design was to get a feeling that pastel art can bring, focusing on light, bubbly colors.

Meant to Pop!

Pastel Pop is intended to stand out from the many sparkling water makers that produce products for the market today. A solid design choice was essential to meet marketing strategies.

Tastes Like it Looks

Pastel Pop tastes exactly like the can looks, sweet and simple!
